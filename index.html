About Our Academy

Our Mission: Building Future Leaders

The PTC Training Academy is committed to providing comprehensive and cutting-edge training for all ranks, from constables to senior officers.

We focus on developing not just physical prowess and tactical skills, but also fostering a deep understanding of law, ethics, and community engagement. Our curriculum is designed to meet the evolving challenges of modern law enforcement, ensuring our trainees are prepared for any situation.

With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced instructors, we aim to instill discipline, integrity, and a strong sense of duty in every individual who walks through our doors.

Our Core Training Modules

Physical Training

Rigorous physical conditioning, endurance building, obstacle courses, and self-defense techniques.

Parade & Discipline

Precision drill, parade commands, ceremonial duties, and fostering unwavering discipline.

Indoor Classes

In-depth study of Indian Penal Code (IPC), Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC), evidence act, police ethics, and cybercrime.
